About

Hi! I’m but a humble writer who’s been writing since I was a child, which means I’ve been at it for over two decades. It truly is a passion of mine. I’ve dabbled in just about all forms of it from script writing to articles. I even taught myself how to make the maps you see just so that I could have an easier time writing campaigns for ttrpgs. I also consider myself an amateur philosopher. Hopefully that explains the topics you’ll see in the works I’ve written. Which brings me to my pen name ALR. I came up with it as a way to explain how I view myself. I don’t want to give away too much though. Got to leave some mystery for you guys right?
